Spaghetti Meat Sauce

Description

This is a simple tomato based meat sauce, which goes with any hot pasta. Spaghetti is a classic paring.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Medium onion, chopped
  • 4 minced cloves of garlic
  • Diced small green bell pepper
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black peper

Steps

  1. Combine beef, onion, garlic, and green pepper in large saucepan over medium-high heat.
  2. Stir until meat is brown and crumbly, and vegetables are tender.
  3. Stir diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste into beef mixture.
  4. Add oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Simmer until think and aromatic.
