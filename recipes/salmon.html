Baked Salmon

Description

This is a very straightforward, easy, and tasty way to bake salmon.

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillet
  • Olive oil
  • Cracked black pepper
  • Salt
  • Minced garlic
  • Italian herb seasoning blend
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees and grease a large baking pan.
  2. Arrange salmon fillets on pan and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Stir together olive oil, garlic, herbs, and juice of half the lemon.
  4. Spoon sauce over salmon fillets, rubbing over top and sides to leave no dry spots.
  5. Thinly slice other half of lemon and top each fillet with a slice.
