Smashburger

Description

Smashburgers are a kind of burger made by smashing a ball of ground beef against the pan or griddle. This gives them a great sear, making the patties flavorful and juicy.

Ingredients

  • Hamburger buns
  • Butter
  • Ground beef
  • Salt
  • American cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat a cast iron griddle or skillet until smoking
  2. Spread butter on inside of the buns and toast until lightly browned
  3. Form meat into loosely-packed ball, 2 ounces
  4. Place ball on flat-top, then smash them down using a spatula
  5. Grill for around 45 seconds, then scrape up the patty and flip
  6. Cover with a slice of cheese, grill for an additional 20 seconds
  7. Move the patty to a bun and serve
